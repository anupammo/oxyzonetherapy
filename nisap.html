HealthCare Product

MODEL NISA–P

NISA-P : This Analog product was the first Medical Ozone Generator to be ever made by an Indian research organization. It has been a historical product and is still upgraded every year since it is still purchased by senior medical practitioners globally. Vintage product - yielding modern success.

Features

  • Patented Technology
  • High Success Rates, certified by senior medical practitioners
  • Perfect Calibration
  • Tropical-Climate Compatible
  • Multi-layer Pitted-through-hole PCB's
  • Nominal Power Consumption
  • Ozone Regulation as per treatment : Continuous regulation in the range of 5-95 micrograms per ml (can be regulated by each microgram per ml)
  • Can be used With / Without timer
  • Functioning Indicators
  • Calibrated for usage with both Air and pure Oxygen

Technical Details

  • Dimensions : 455X255X210 mm
  • Weight : 10 Kg
  • Medical Ozone : 5-95 micrograms per ml
  • Noise : 48 decibels
  • Power Consumption : 85 Watts
  • Recommended Oxygen flow : 1-2 LPM
  • No harmful by-products
